QUESTION: The blue book is to the right of the yellow book. The orange book is the second from the left.
OPTIONS: (a) "The orange book is the rightmost", (b) "The yellow book is the rightmost", (c) "The blue book is the rightmost"
ANSWER: (c)

ANSWER: (c)
